APJ Abdul Kalam was an aeronautical engineer who graduated from Madras Institute of Technology in the year 1960. APJ Abdul Kalam is also known as Missile Man. APJ Abdul Kalam was honored with the Bharat Ratna Award in the year 1977. APJ Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India
